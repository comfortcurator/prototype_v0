# User Personas

## Host: Riya Mehta
- **Profile:** 32, manages 4 premium Airbnb listings in Goa and Mumbai; ex-corporate professional turned hospitality entrepreneur.
- **Goals:** Maintain 5-star cleanliness, minimize downtime between stays, keep inventory costs predictable.
- **Pain Points:** Tracking cleaners, coordinating suppliers, reconciling expenses, and manual Razorpay payment links.
- **Needs:** Mobile-first dashboard, real-time alerts, predefined supply kits, GST invoices, Hindi support for staff.
- **Success Signs:** Reduced turnaround time, automated reordering, centralized property analytics.

## Host: Kabir Singh
- **Profile:** 44, owns boutique homestay chain in Himachal; operates remotely with on-site caretakers.
- **Goals:** Ensure consistent guest experience despite remote location; manage supplies in bulk.
- **Pain Points:** Unreliable connectivity, manual WhatsApp updates, lack of visibility into stock usage.
- **Needs:** Offline-capable PWA, caretaker access with permissions, automated SMS notifications, backup package automation.
- **Success Signs:** Low stock alerts before incidents, transparent caretaker accountability, streamlined procurement.

## Admin: Ananya Desai
- **Profile:** 29, operations manager overseeing supplier relations and inventory for 200 properties.
- **Goals:** Optimize supplier contracts, maintain accurate stock levels, grow subscription conversions.
- **Pain Points:** Fragmented tools, manual spreadsheets, slow reporting cycles, ad-hoc compliance tracking.
- **Needs:** Comprehensive admin console, analytics with drill-down, audit-proof inventory sessions, automated reports.
- **Success Signs:** Higher package uptake, reduced stockouts, faster reconciliation, clear incident traceability.

## Admin: Rahul Verma
- **Profile:** 38, finance controller responsible for revenue recognition, GST filings, and payment reconciliation.
- **Goals:** Ensure accurate payments, compliance-ready documentation, and minimised chargebacks.
- **Pain Points:** Manual Razorpay settlement matching, inconsistent invoice formats, data silos.
- **Needs:** Razorpay webhook integrity, unified invoice templates, exportable financial reports, role-based approvals.
- **Success Signs:** Automated reconciliation, zero compliance escalations, predictable monthly reports.
