## Post-Launch Growth Roadmap

### Growth & Retention
- Tiered concierge add-ons (linen laundering, regional gifting).
- Loyalty programme for hosts with referral bonuses.
- Partner marketplace integrations (local suppliers, cleaning crews).

### Data & Insights
- Advanced analytics: occupancy vs. spend correlation, demand forecasting.
- Cohort analysis dashboards and Mixpanel funnels.
- Experimentation framework for pricing (A/B test subscription tiers).

### Mobile
- Expo-powered companion app (push notifications, offline checklist).
- Deep linking from push -> property health or order tracking.
- Camera workflows for receipt uploads, inventory scanning.

### Automation Enhancements
- IoT sensor integrations (smart locks, energy usage) feeding health logs.
- AI-driven stock predictions using historic consumption + seasonality.
- Automated vendor reordering with invoice reconciliation.

### Compliance & Ops
- GST invoice automation, TDS reconciliation.
- SLA dashboards for concierge partners; contract management module.
- Quarterly security reviews & dependency upgrades schedule.
